Behind every photo, every moment, there are people who work with heart to bring all of your imagination to reality. Do you want to know who they are?

Based on the hope that all the greatest, sweetest moments of your new beginning can be captured as real as possible, we, VITA NOVA team gathered and decided to be your Fairy God Mother. By drawing on your personal style and working with your venue setting, we specialize in a classically elegant, timeless aesthetic that creates atmosphere and wows your guests. From abundant flowers and flickering candlelight to draping table linens and shimmering glassware, we’re all about subtle luxury that stands the test of time.

WHAT MAKES US STAND OUT?

ATTENTIVENESS

We want you to look back on your wedding day in 20 years and never question your decisions. We are here to be your friend when you most need it. To be in your corner when you’re trying to keep everyone happy. And to help you create the most incredible, personal wedding without the stress factor.

SIMPLICITY

We believe in the beauty of simplicity and clarity. Furthermore, we believe that the more simple, the more perfect your wedding will be. Because beauty doesn’t need enhancement — it just is. It’s about telling your love story in your own unique way, and to us, that’s the ultimate in romance.

CRAZINESS

It all starts with you. Your personalities. Your love. Your vision of your wedding. Even if they are the craziest, wildest, silliest dreams. We’ll create an experience you’ll treasure forever. You don’t need to be scared of dreaming crazily, because we are crazy too. Let your magical love spread out.

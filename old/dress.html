Allure Bridals is all about the details — beading, floral appliqués, dramatic backs, and perfect draping. Each gown features details to set it apart and make the bride shine. The Allure Bridals collection features a series of gorgeous, innovative fabrics — with unique crepe, soft Mikado skirts and a number of amazing stretch linings incorporated into the latest collections. Our design team pared down the silhouettes and detailing for many of our gowns to highlight the texture, drape, beauty and versatility of our new fabrics.
